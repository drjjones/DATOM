How DATOM works

DATOM is not another AI system that produces answers. It is a structural layer that makes claims, evidence, and uncertainty explicit—so AI can move faster without hidden reasoning.

Instead of optimizing for fluency, DATOM optimizes for defensibility. Teams can trace conclusions to specific evidence units, see dependencies, and update reasoning when new information arrives.

Process

A pipeline that converts source material into structures that can be examined, compared, challenged, and improved over time—by people and by machines.

01

Ingest evidence

Preserve source material as a first-class object with provenance, context, and retrieval intact.

02

Extract Datoms

Decompose evidence into atomic, assessable claims that can be evaluated independently.

03

Build Datomers

Relate claims through agreement, contradiction, and gaps to see what a conclusion depends on.

04

Interrogate

Ask questions against explicit evidence structures. Outputs remain inspectable across teams and time.

What this enables

DATOM turns AI outputs into reviewable reasoning artifacts, ensuring you gain speed without losing control.
