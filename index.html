The Problem

Important decisions are frequently made before certainty exists. While evidence accumulates unevenly, institutions often lack an auditable representation of that evidence state, relying instead on informal inference. This loss of context ensures that hindsight bias eventually replaces true accountability.

Without a shared ledger of evidence structure, institutional memory degrades and risk becomes unmeasurable.

DATOM vs. Generative AI

DATOM is not an AI assistant. While Large Language Models prioritize generating plausible answers, DATOM maintains the structural state of evidence. It does not recommend actions; it makes the basis for action legible.

Mechanism

Confidence

A property of evidence structure, derived from the density, replication, and agreement of underlying atomic claims.

Decision Readiness

An explicit threshold indicating when the current evidence structure supports a specific operational commitment.

Next-Best Evidence

The specific data or replication required to resolve the highest remaining uncertainty for a given claim.

Application

  • Researchers: Manage claim provenance and preserve disagreement as data.
  • Founders: Demonstrate technical maturity bands to stakeholders and boards.
  • Investors: Audit the structural integrity of technical claims during diligence.
  • Policymakers: Anchor regulatory frameworks in auditable evidence states.
